  • Internet Explorers, 2019—ongoing
    teaching

    Workshops for beginners on how to make websites using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Participants use Beaker Browser publish their sites on the peer-to-peer web. We also look critically at the internet, discussing surveillance and the impact of global internet infrastructure.

  • Galeries Lafayette Paris, 2019
    sound design • UI design

    Designed interface sounds for a holding page for the flagship Galeries Lafayette department store in Paris. Design by DVTK, development by Jack Wild.

  • Device Phase, 2016 (documentation video)
    music composition • development • interaction design • performance

    In this music composition, audience members become performers and their smartphones become instruments. Differences between each phone's system clock create spatial effects in the room. Notification sounds—normally forbidden in a concert setting—contribute to the piece.
